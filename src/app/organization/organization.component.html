Welcome to No-To-Hunger


No-To-Hunger is a web-based application that enables organizations/ individuals to donate leftover food
to the people/ groups in need and the application allows volunteers to offer service such as delivering
food from donating organizations to needy people. United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) reports
that food loss occurs at every stage of the production and supply chain. On a day-to-day basis, vast amount
of fresh food gets expelled from household,restaurants and social events. Food wastage is regarded as a
global issue, this application aims at restricting food wastage and enable institutions to donate food to
needy people. Multiple organization wish to donate food can use this application to make their capable
contributions and the application also allows groups/individuals to put in their food requests.
